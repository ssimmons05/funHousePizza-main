About



OUR STORY

David Rushton opened Fun House Pizza on February 11, 1968. We are family owned and are an independent chain. We have been serving the people of the Kansas City metro area for over 50 years.

OUR PURPOSE

Our objective has been the same since we first opened our doors. We want our customers to enjoy great pizza at a family orient location. A place where families can come together. A place where kids can celebrate a victory after a local sports event or celebrate at a birthday party. Parents can sit back and enjoy an ice cold beer to wash down with our delicious pizza.

OUR VALUES

We value tradition here at Fun House Pizza. Not much has changed since we first opened over 50 years ago and I'm sure that many of our cutomers like it that way. Loyal customers who grew up visiting our restaraunt as children are now bringing their children here. Parents can look back fondly on old times while their kids sit on the same seats or ride the same mechanical horses as they did as a child. Our great tasting pizza is still hand made and cooked in the same ovens that they were over 50 years ago.
